🌟 Our Approach

All sessions are designed with sensory-loaded, hands-on activities that make learning engaging and effective for children with moderate to severe autism. We incorporate techniques aligned with ABA therapy, speech therapy, OT, and PT goals — so our sessions complement your child's existing support team.
